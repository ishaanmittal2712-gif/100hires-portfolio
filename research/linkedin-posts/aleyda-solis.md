## Post 1 — Launching LearningAISearch.com (July 2025)
**Source:** https://firstpagesage.com/seo-blog/top-geo-consultants

dedicated entirely to AI search optimization education.

Context: She identified a gap in the market — most GEO content was 
either too theoretical or too vendor-driven. Practitioners needed a

- Multilingual and international GEO (her primary specialty)
- Practical frameworks for optimizing content for LLM citation
