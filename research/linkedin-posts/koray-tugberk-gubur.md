## Post 1 — Topical Authority as the Foundation of AI SEO
**Source:** https://digitalmarketingsupermarket.com/blog/top-24-ai-seo-expert-marketers-on-linkedin-to-follow-in-2026/
**Source 2:** https://www.ewrdigital.com/blog/most-helpful-seo-aeo-influencers-to-follow

Koray is the creator of the Topical Authority methodology — one of 
the most referenced frameworks in SEO in 2024-26.

Core argument: Search engines and AI systems don't just evaluate 
individual pages. They evaluate the entire topical ecosystem of a 
website — whether a site comprehensively covers a subject area 
determines how much it is trusted and cited.

Key concepts he introduced to mainstream SEO:
- Topical Authority: Building comprehensive coverage of a subject 
  area so search engines recognize your site as the definitive 
  source on that topic
- Semantic SEO: Structuring content around concepts and entities, 
  not just keywords
- Entity-based content: Helping AI systems understand what your 
  content is about through clear entity relationships
- AI content workflows: Using AI at specific stages of content 
  production without compromising depth and expertise

Published work: 87-lecture Semantic SEO Course, SEO Mastery Summit 
organizer, Fortune 500 consulting.

**Annotation:** Koray's Topical Authority framework has become the 
dominant content architecture philosophy for serious SEO practitioners 
in 2025-26. For B2B SaaS companies like 100Hires, building topical 
authority around hiring, ATS, and recruitment content is the most 
defensible long-term SEO strategy — especially as AI systems 
increasingly reward comprehensive subject coverage over individual 
optimized pages.
