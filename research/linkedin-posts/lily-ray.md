## Post 1 — Your GEO Strategy Might Be Destroying Your SEO (March 2026)
**Source:** https://lilyraynyc.substack.com/p/your-geo-strategy-might-be-destroying

Key argument: Several popular GEO tactics are already being treated as 
spam by Google and Microsoft. Marketers scaling those techniques face 
genuine risk of long-term algorithmic penalties.

Specific tactics she flags as dangerous:
- Scaling AI-generated content without editorial oversight
- Self-promotional listicles published at scale (she found companies 
  with 2,000+ articles all claiming to be #1 in their category)
- Prompt injection via "summarize with AI" buttons
- Separate markdown pages created purely for AI crawlers

Key data point: On January 20, 2026, she observed dozens of companies 
getting hit by what appeared to be a targeted Google action — almost 
unanimously, all had published hundreds or thousands of listicles.

Core thesis: "A pattern I keep seeing in AI search success stories is 
that the GEO wins being celebrated can almost always be explained by 
something simpler: strong pre-existing SEO performance."

**Annotation:** This is the most important counterpoint voice in AI SEO 
right now. While most experts talk about how to win with AI content, 
Lily documents what's already getting penalized. For a B2B SaaS company 
like 100Hires building a long-term content moat, her warnings are more 
strategically valuable than any growth hack.

## Post 2 — State of AI and SEO in 2026 (Keynote, Affiliate Summit West)
**Source:** https://www.affiliatesummit.com/blogs/the-state-of-ai-and-seo-in-2026-with-lily-ray

Key takeaways from her keynote:
- Search engines are evolving into answer engines
- Clicks will decline; brand visibility becomes the new KPI
- SEO still powers AI — ranking influences RAG citations
- Third-party reputation is critical — AI trusts corroboration
- Multimodal content wins — YouTube and social are heavily cited
- "The era of answer engines is here. If you want your brand 
  recommended, cited, and trusted inside AI search results — 
  NOW is the time to adapt."

**Annotation:** Delivered to affiliate marketers but directly applicable 
to B2B SaaS. The shift from traffic KPIs to brand visibility KPIs is 
something every growth team at a SaaS company needs to internalize now.
